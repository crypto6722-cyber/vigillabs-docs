# Pricing & Plans

VigiLabs is a subscription service. A single subscription unlocks all chains, unlimited callers, and full execution rules. This page explains the plans, the free trial, and promo codes.

:::note Check current pricing in the app
Prices, plan lengths, and promotions can change. The figures below reflect the general structure — always confirm the current price and terms on the subscribe screen in the app before paying.
:::

## Plans

VigiLabs offers subscription plans of different lengths, with the longer plans working out cheaper per month. Every plan unlocks the same full feature set — all chains, unlimited callers, and complete take-profit and stop-loss rules. The difference between plans is only the length and the effective monthly rate.

## Free trial

You can start with a free trial before paying anything. During the trial you get access to the app to try the service out, with some limits compared to a full subscription. No payment is taken to start a trial, and you can convert to a paid plan when you're ready.

When a trial ends without converting, automated trading and group management stop, but you keep access to view and withdraw your funds — those are never gated.

## Promo codes

If you joined through a caller group that has a promo code, you can enter it on the subscribe screen. A valid code applies a discount to your first paid charge. You enter the code in the app when subscribing — the discounted amount is what gets charged.

## Paying

VigiLabs takes payment through its integrated crypto payment checkout. When you subscribe, you're taken to the checkout to complete payment, and once it's confirmed your full access is unlocked.

## What happens if my subscription lapses

If your subscription expires, you stay logged in and keep full access to **view and withdraw your funds** — VigiLabs never blocks access to your money. What pauses is automated trading and the ability to add or remove groups. Renew to turn those back on.
