# Welcome to VigiLabs

VigiLabs is an execution service for meme coin signals. It watches the Telegram and Discord channels you already follow, and when a trusted caller posts a token, VigiLabs can buy it automatically — across Solana, Ethereum, Base, BNB Chain, TON, and HyperEVM — using the position size, slippage, and take-profit rules you set in advance.

The idea is simple: signals move fast, and by the time you've seen a call, opened your wallet, and approved a swap, the moment has often passed. VigiLabs closes that gap. You configure your rules once, choose which callers to follow, and the service handles execution while you stay in control of the settings.

## Who it's for

VigiLabs is built for people who already follow meme coin callers and want to act on their signals quickly and consistently, without watching every chat around the clock. You decide which channels to trust, how much to spend, and when to take profit or cut losses. VigiLabs does the mechanical work of executing those decisions.

## What it does, at a glance

- **Listens to your signal groups.** It reads the Telegram chats and Discord channels you choose to follow.
- **Executes automatically.** When a followed caller posts a token, VigiLabs buys it according to your rules.
- **Manages exits.** Take-profit levels and stop-loss rules can close positions for you.
- **Works across chains.** Solana, Ethereum, Base, BNB Chain, TON, and HyperEVM from a single dashboard.
- **Keeps you in control.** You set the rules, choose the callers, and can stop all trading at any time.

## A note before you start

Meme coin trading is high-risk. Prices move violently, many tokens have thin liquidity, and losses can be total. VigiLabs is a tool for executing your own decisions — it is not financial advice, and it cannot protect you from the risks of the assets you choose to trade. Please read the [Security & Custody](./security-and-custody) and [Risk Disclaimer](./risk-disclaimer) pages before you fund an account.
