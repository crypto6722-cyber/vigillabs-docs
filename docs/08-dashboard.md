# Dashboard

Your dashboard is where you monitor everything VigiLabs is doing on your behalf — open positions, recent activity, and your overall performance. Data updates automatically so you can watch trades as they happen.

## Positions

The positions view shows your currently open trades. For each position you can see details such as the token, the chain it's on, your entry, the current price, position size, and how it's performing. This is where you keep an eye on trades that are still live and, if you want, close them manually.

## Activity

The activity feed is a running log of what VigiLabs has done: buys, sells, take-profit hits, stop-loss hits, and the signals it has picked up. Each entry records what happened and when, so you have a clear history of every action taken on your account.

## Stats

The stats view summarizes your performance over time — how your trades have done in aggregate. Use it to review how your settings and chosen callers are working out.

## Wallets and Trading

From the dashboard you can jump to your [Wallets](./wallets) to fund or withdraw, and to [Trading Settings](./trading-settings) to adjust how VigiLabs trades or to stop trading entirely.

## Keeping an eye on things

Automated trading doesn't mean walk-away-and-forget. It's worth checking your dashboard regularly, especially after following a new caller or changing your settings, to make sure VigiLabs is behaving the way you expect.
