# Supported Chains

VigiLabs executes trades across several blockchains. Each chain routes through deep decentralized-exchange (DEX) liquidity so your trades fill at competitive prices. You only need wallets and settings for the chains you actually want to trade.

## Solana

Follow meme coin callers on Solana and trade SPL tokens. Execution routes through Jupiter, which aggregates the deepest liquidity across Solana DEXes for fast fills.

## Ethereum, Base, and BNB Chain (EVM)

VigiLabs trades across the major EVM chains — Ethereum, Base, and BNB Chain — using a single EVM wallet. Trades route automatically through the best available DEX liquidity on each chain. Because these three chains share one EVM wallet, you set your EVM buy amount once and it applies wherever you trade on EVM.

## TON

Trade Jettons on TON. VigiLabs routes through TON's deepest native DEX liquidity automatically.

## HyperEVM

Trade tokens on HyperEVM, including HYPE. As with the other chains, VigiLabs routes through the deepest available DEX liquidity.

## How routing works

For each trade, VigiLabs identifies the token's chain from the signal and executes on the matching wallet — a Solana token trades from your Solana wallet, an EVM token from your EVM wallet, and so on. You don't need to sort signals by chain yourself; VigiLabs handles that automatically based on the token that was posted.

## Enabling chains

A chain only trades when you've enabled it on the Trading page and funded a wallet for it. You can run just one chain or several — it's entirely up to you. See [Trading Settings](./trading-settings) for how to enable and disable chains.
